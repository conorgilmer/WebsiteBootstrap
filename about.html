About
The about section of the app give an overview of the application and provides useful information on the author, social context, word glossaries and other tidbits and fun facts
Start
  • Login and Start using email or social media login
  • Allows user to begin a tour (self-guided, audio-based or through tour operator)
  • Allows user see other users previous and ongoing tours
  • Provide user with introductory info regarding the tour
Tours
  • List Tours and Events around the theme of Bloomsday
  • Link to a map of each suggested Tour
  • Links to various tour operators
  • Option to begin a self-guided tour
  • Option to "Begin a new tour" or Continue current tour"
Places
  • List of places featured in Ulysses
  • Map showing the places featured
  • Information regarding the place and its relevance to Ulysses and James Joyce
  • Gives the users information on the nearest places of interest
Track
  • Integration with google maps to provide GPS location and Userâ€™s Route
  • Provides zoom functionality
  • Allows users to toggle between lists of places and map view
  • A tagging feature allowing users to tag themselves at places of interest
Others
  • Users can view routes saved by otherMyBloomsday users
  • An option to take the routes saved by other users
  • An option to invite people to join in on an existing tour via social media
  • Allows users to check if there are other users in the area
Share
    Users Can
  • Upload their track to their social media
  • Upload their photos and videos to their social media
  • Recommend the app to their friends and post feedback and reviews
Finish
  • Allows user to finalise and save his/her Bloomsday experience
  • Offers users the option to share their experience via social media websites
